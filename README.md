# 🥚 EggsSharer App

EggsSharer is a completely free, offline-capable Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for backyard chicken owners to intuitively manage their flock's production.

### Features
* **Harvest Tracking:** Log daily egg collection and view historical averages.
* **Sales & CRM:** Keep track of your neighbors and customers, log sales or gifts, and send rapid pre-filled WhatsApp share messages for pick-ups.
* **Cost Management:** Record food, litter, and medical expenses.
* **Insightful Reports:** Interactive charts (using Chart.js) that map out your net profit, inventory stock levels, and revenue flows over time.
* **Bilingual:** Instantly toggle between English and French.
* **100% Private:** No database or backend. Everything is stored purely in your device's Local Storage.

### Usage
Visit the live site on your phone, open the browser menu, and tap **"Add to Home Screen"** to install it as a fully native-feeling app that works without an internet connection!
